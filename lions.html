Project Overview

The Rakaia Lions Club needed a better way to run their annual lily bulb fundraiser. For years, sales relied on paper flyers and manual orders, but this approach limited their reach, required a lot of administration, and made it harder to connect with a broader customer base. With more people shopping online, there was a clear opportunity to modernise the process and make purchasing more convenient, accessible, and engaging for the community.

The creation of a dedicated e-commerce website for lily bulb sales . The site was designed to be intuitive and easy to navigate, allowing customers to browse bulb varieties, see high-quality images, and place orders in just a few clicks. By replacing the old flyer system with an online platform, the fundraiser now reaches beyond the local area, helping the Lions Club connect with new audiences while simplifying the experience for existing supporters.
